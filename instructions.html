How to Play cosmicDash

Navigate through space, collect power-ups, and achieve the highest score!

Game Controls

  • Tap to Jump - Double tap for higher jumps
  • Swipe left/right to change lanes
  • Collect stars for power-ups and bonus points
  • Build combos by collecting items in sequence

Scoring System

  • Basic Obstacle Avoided: 10 points
  • Energy Orb Collected: 25 points
  • Power-up Collected: 50 points
  • Perfect Combo Bonus: +100 points

Visual Elements

  • Player Ship: Blue with glowing effect
  • Obstacles: Red with warning glow
  • Power-ups: Golden with pulsing effect
  • Energy Orbs: Green with floating animation
  • Combo Boosters: Magenta with electric effect
  • Shield Power-up: Cyan with protective aura

Pro Tips

  • Time your jumps carefully to avoid obstacles
  • Collect power-ups to become invincible temporarily
  • Maintain your combo multiplier for higher scores
  • Watch out for increasing difficulty as you progress
  • Use the joystick for precise movement control
